The Tortoise and the Hare

Aesop

Illustrated by: Jeanelle S. Yee

Narrated by: Chelsy B. Jimeno

Original URL: americanliterature.com

In the classic tale of "The Hare and the Tortoise," a boastful hare challenges a slow-moving tortoise to a race. The hare, confident in his speed, takes an early lead, while the tortoise plods along steadily. Who do you think will win the race: the speedy hare or the steady tortoise?
